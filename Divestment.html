Divestment Services

What Is Divestment?

Divestment refers to the process of withdrawing investments from industries, assets, or companies that contribute to environmental harm, particularly those involved in fossil fuels. At BayMount Power, we help businesses transition from non-renewable energy sources to sustainable alternatives.

Our Divestment Strategy

  • Review Your Energy Portfolio: We conduct a thorough assessment of your current energy consumption and identify where fossil fuel divestment can be most impactful.
  • Customized Divestment Plan: We design a step-by-step plan to help your company move away from non-renewable energy sources while maintaining operational efficiency.
  • Implementation & Monitoring: BayMount Power assists with implementing divestment strategies and monitors progress to ensure a smooth and sustainable transition to renewable energy.

Why Divest from Fossil Fuels?

  • Lower Environmental Impact: Reducing reliance on fossil fuels helps lower your carbon footprint and contributes to global sustainability efforts.
  • Cost Savings: Renewable energy solutions often provide long-term cost savings over traditional energy sources.
