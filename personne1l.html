Meet Our Team

Our Experienced Professionals

At BayMount Power, our team consists of experienced professionals dedicated to providing innovative energy solutions. Each member of our team brings unique expertise, ensuring our clients receive the highest level of service.

Patrick (Pat) Goodale, President, Principal, & Founder

Under the leadership of Patrick (Pat) Goodale, BayMount Power has flourished, leveraging his deep industry experience to drive innovation and success. With over 15 years in the energy sector, Pat’s strategic vision and problem-solving abilities have set the foundation for the company’s growth.

Key Accomplishments

  • Industry Experience: Over 15 years in government, utilities, and project development.
  • Project Leadership: Spearheaded four project sales campaigns totaling more than 150MW in capacity.
  • Expertise: Solar development expertise across five North American jurisdictions.

Pat prides himself on being a problem-solver with strong collaborative skills, able to effectively communicate with diverse stakeholders. His success in the energy industry is complemented by his educational achievements, holding a Master’s in Economics from Toronto Metropolitan University, an MBA, and an Honours Bachelor of Commerce degree in Finance from McMaster University.

Pat’s Vision

Pat’s passion for renewable energy extends beyond business—he’s driven by a commitment to building a sustainable future and helping companies make responsible energy choices. His leadership at BayMount Power is a testament to his dedication to innovation and sustainable solutions.

"I believe the future of energy lies in responsible and innovative solutions that not only benefit businesses but the environment as well. At BayMount Power, we’re proud to lead the way in shaping this future." — Patrick Goodale
