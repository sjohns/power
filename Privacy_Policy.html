Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters to Us

At BayMount Power, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and the rights you have regarding your data. By using our website or services, you consent to the data collection practices described in this Privacy Policy.

What Information Do We Collect?

We collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): This includes your name, email address, phone number, and other details you provide directly to us.
  • Technical Data: Including your IP address, browser type, and browsing behavior, which may be collected automatically when you use our website through cookies and other tracking technologies.

How Do We Use Your Information?

  • To provide you with the services and support you request from BayMount Power.
  • To communicate important updates or changes to our services.
  • To improve our website and services based on customer feedback.
  • To comply with legal obligations and safeguard our rights.
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior to improve our services (through tools such as Google Analytics).
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted service providers (e.g., payment processors) to fulfill the services we offer.

How Do We Protect Your Data?

We use SSL/TLS encryption to protect data during transmission, and we ensure that all sensitive data is encrypted at rest. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we regularly conduct security audits to detect vulnerabilities and ensure the integrity of our systems.

How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Once your data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data and ask for a copy of it.
  • Correction: If any of your information is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request corrections.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Compliance with GDPR and CCPA

We strive to comply with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in the United States. If you are located in the European Union or California, you may have additional rights under these regulations, including the right to request the erasure of your personal data or to opt-out of data collection practices.

If you have any concerns regarding your rights under GDPR or CCPA, please contact us.

Do We Share Your Information?

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers to assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you. This includes:

  • Payment processors
  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing service providers

We ensure that these parties are contractually obligated to protect your personal information in accordance with this policy and applicable privacy laws.

What Happens in Case of a Data Breach?

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within a reasonable timeframe and take all necessary steps to mitigate the impact. This includes informing relevant regulatory authorities and providing guidance on how users can protect their information.

Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us or adjusting your browser settings to block cookies or tracking technologies.

International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than the country in which you are resident. These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy is effective as of September 8, 2024 and may be updated from time to time. Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of any significant changes either via email or through a prominent notice on our website.

Have Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at any time. We are committed to maintaining transparency and addressing any issues you may have.
